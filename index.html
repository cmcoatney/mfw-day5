Comic Book Page Layout

Gutters, Margins & Spacing Practice

Comic book pages have a predictable layout: A margin around the whole page that is taller at the bottom. A page number centered at the bottom immediately below the panel borders. And various panels with a gutter size usually a bit less than the outer margin.
